Should I visit Australia next?

Australia is my number one DREAM destination. I've always had an obsession with Australia ever since I was a teenager. That might have had something to do with the fact that my favourite band was from Australia... but that's neither here nor there. Something about a place that isn't -40 degrees in the winter is very appealing to me. Their beaches are so stunning. I could spend all day at them.

They have such sweet critters that you can't find anywhere else. Also, I'm not one for thrilling experiences, but even I think I would enjoy going scuba diving in their clear water.

Like, stop procrastinating mate... just go to Australia. Chan approves.
